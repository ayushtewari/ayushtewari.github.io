Research Focus

The primary goal of my lab is to build world models that can capture the physical structure of the world from image and video observations and enable complex physical reasoning. While I am open to a broad range of ideas within this domain, I am particularly interested in students keen to explore topics such as:

  • Physical inductive biases for world models
  • Robust and explainable inference and reasoning with generative models
  • Control and interpretability of large generative models

If your interests align with this research direction, I encourage you to apply and mention my name in your application.
