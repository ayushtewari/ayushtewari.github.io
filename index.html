Ayush Tewari

ayusht@mit.edu

I am a postdoctoral researcher at MIT CSAIL with Bill Freeman, Josh Tenenbaum, and Vincent Sitzmann. I completed my Ph.D. at the Max Planck Institute for Informatics with Christian Theobalt, and I continue to be associated with the institute as a researcher. My research develops methods for perceiving and interacting with the visual world from sparse observations. Check out some recent talks for an overview.

I am on the faculty job market this year.
